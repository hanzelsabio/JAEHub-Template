Cash on Delivery Philippines

In the Philippines, Cash on Delivery is a widely used payment method. Even with the increased acceptance of e-payments throughout the years, Cash on Delivery has proven to be a convenient payment option that’s here to stay.

JAEHub believes that businesses who want to tap the Philippine E-commerce market, whether local or international, should consider how they can offer Cash on Delivery to their customers. That’s why we provide Cash on Delivery Integration!

If you’re still unfamiliar with the concept of Cash on Delivery, here’s a quick rundown on what it is and how JAEHub helps merchants offer it.

Defining Cash on Delivery

Cash on Delivery is a transaction wherein customers pay for products or goods at the time when they are delivered to them. It’s usually the courier rider who receives the customer’s payment, and handles turning it over to JAEHub once all deliveries for the day are done.

A lot of Filipinos still remain unbanked, so providing Cash on Delivery in the Philippines is an important aspect of adapting to local consumer behavior. Cash on Delivery gives customers more flexibility in terms of how they pay for the products they buy online, making E-commerce more accessible to the country as a whole.

JAEHub’s Cash on Delivery Integration

Merchants, especially those who use Shopify for their E-commerce platform, can easily offer Cash on Delivery as a payment option through JAEHub’s Cash on Delivery plug-in. Their customers will automatically see the option for Cash on Delivery upon check out

The different couriers that JAEHub works with are the ones who initially receive the Cash on Delivery payments. They will then remit it to us so that we can take care of remitting it to our merchants. Merchants who want to get their remittance right away can opt for our Early Remittance, wherein we give our merchants their remittance even before our couriers remit them.

With JAEHub, offering Cash on Delivery and getting Cash on Delivery payments from the Philippines doesn’t have to be hard.
